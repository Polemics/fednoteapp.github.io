Our App

At FedNote, we're dedicated to empowering federal employees facing workforce transitions. We've built an essential tool that cuts through administrative complexity, helps workers document important events, and improves overall career protection. Our comprehensive documentation and tracking solution streamlines the process of recording workplace events, managing communications, and preparing for potential proceedings.

FedNote is designed with real-world feedback, continuously refined based on federal worker experiences, and built on a foundation of trust, utility, and respect for your privacy and professional needs. Whether you're a senior employee or new to federal service, our app gives you the tools to protect your career and document what matters most.

Key Features

• Detailed event logging and timeline tracking
• Secure document scanning and storage
• Communications record keeping
• Sworn declaration support
• Comprehensive preparation checklists
• Complete offline functionality

Privacy First

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. FedNote operates completely offline, ensuring all your sensitive information stays solely on your device. No cloud storage, no data collection, no external connections - just secure, local documentation you can trust.
