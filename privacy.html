Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the use of software applications ("Applications") developed by FedNote, including all related tools and services.

Overview

At FedNote, we take privacy seriously. Our Applications are designed as documentation tools for federal workers, with a focus on complete data privacy and security. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data from these Applications.

User Provided Information

No personal information is collected. Any data you enter into the Application remains solely on your device and is never transmitted to us or shared with third parties. If an Application includes functionality that allows you to generate documents or emails, that information is only used locally within your device and not collected by us.
