Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need FedNote?

FedNote helps you maintain detailed documentation during federal workforce transitions, ensuring you have organized, comprehensive records for potential future proceedings.

Is my data secure?

FedNote operates completely offline with no cloud connectivity. All your data stays locally on your device, giving you complete control over your information.

What types of documentation can I store?

FedNote supports various documentation types including meeting notes, communications logs, official notices, photos, scanned documents, and sworn declarations.
